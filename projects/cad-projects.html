CAD Projects

These are small CAD projects I worked on primarily for fun and skill-building while learning SolidWorks and Siemens NX. Most were created outside of any formal product requirement—just exploring geometry, constraints, assemblies, and clean modeling practices.

The focus here wasn’t originality or performance, but getting comfortable translating ideas into fully defined models, understanding how parts fit together, and building intuition for manufacturable geometry.
